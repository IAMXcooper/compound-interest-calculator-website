Understanding Compound Interest

Albert Einstein reportedly called compound interest the "eighth wonder of the world." Unlike simple interest, which is calculated only on the principal amount, compound interest is calculated on the principal plus the accumulated interest.

üìà Exponential Growth

Your money grows faster over time because your interest earns interest. The longer you leave your money invested, the more powerful this effect becomes.

‚è≥ Time is Key

Starting early is often more important than starting big. A small amount invested for a long time can outgrow a large amount invested for a shorter time.
